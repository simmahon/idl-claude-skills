## Who Ideal Direct is

Ideal Direct Ltd is a UK-based multi-brand Amazon FBA and eCommerce company. The business operates nine consumer-goods brands across several categories and sells primarily through Amazon marketplaces and direct eCommerce channels.

The company's commercial model: own the brand, own the listing, source from third-party factories (mainly in China), ship via FBA and direct fulfilment, and compete on product quality and listing craft rather than price alone.

## The nine brands

Every output must be consistent with whichever brand it is produced for. The brand list:

- **Rooted Human**
- **DIY Doctor**
- **Deer & Oak**
- **Tydi**
- **Yogii**
- **Fly-Bye**
- **iMedic**
- **Medipaq**
- **Pest X Pro**

When a brief doesn't name a brand, ask which brand the output is for before building anything. The tone, category, and compliance posture differ per brand.

## Product categories

Products across the nine brands span:

- Kitchenware
- Pest control
- Yoga and wellness
- Health supplements
- Household goods
- Medical-adjacent products

## Markets

- **United Kingdom** — primary market
- **European Union** — Germany, France, and others
- **United States** — secondary and growing

Default a document's target market to the UK unless the brief says otherwise. If a document applies to multiple markets, call that out explicitly on the cover and in the content (regulations, claims, and compliance vary by market).

## Sales channels

- Amazon FBA across UK, EU and US marketplaces
- Direct eCommerce (brand-owned storefronts)

Multi-brand, multi-marketplace is the default assumption. Documents should never assume a single-channel context.

## Commercial sensitivities (applies across roles)

Two product categories carry strict Amazon restrictions and require careful claims management regardless of which role the assistant is supporting:

- **Pest control** — category-restricted on Amazon; claims and labelling are scrutinised.
- **Health supplements** — category-restricted; efficacy claims are tightly controlled.

If a task touches either category and the brief is vague about compliance posture or target market, raise the question during the Step 1 Gather of the confirmation flow (see `confirmation-flow-pattern.md`).
