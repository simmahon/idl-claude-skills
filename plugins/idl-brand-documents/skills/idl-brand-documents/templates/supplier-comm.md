# Template — Supplier Communication

**Cover label:** `SUPPLIER COMMUNICATION`
**Cover title:** factory name + subject (e.g. "Ningbo Factory — Q2 PO Quality Conditions")
**Cover meta items:** Factory · Recipient · Date

## Content
- Section: Recipient Details — `card-grid` (3 cards: factory, primary contact, role)
- Section: Background / Context — short paragraphs (no list, prose only — this is the "why")
- Section: Requirements — `data-table` (Item, Specification, Deadline)
- Section: Key Conditions — `info-box` with vermillion border (the non-negotiables)
- Section: Next Steps — `bullet-list` (what we expect from them, by when)

## Tone
Professional, firm, collaborative. Clear on what is needed and by when. No casual phrasing — these documents sometimes accompany money owed or PO conditions.

## Notes
- Deadlines in Requirements: ISO date (`2026-05-30`) or specific phrase ("on receipt of next PO").
- Background: 1–3 short paragraphs max. Don't pad. The supplier's time is also valuable.
- If a Requirement carries a financial implication, state it in Key Conditions, not the table.
